Tips for Creating Offices

  • Office Name: Use the full, official name of the department or office
  • Office Code: Keep it short and memorable (2-10 characters)
  • Parent Office: Create departments first, then add sub-offices under them
  • Color: Choose distinct colors to help differentiate offices in calendars and reports
